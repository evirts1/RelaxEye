Eye exercise and relaxer.

Use of the eye exerciser is at your own risk. Stop using the eye exerciser if you experience any pain or discomfort.
This page allows you to simulate focusing your eyes very close and very far away.
Near vision procedure:
Click on the screen to activate image movement. The number indicates how far apart the images are.
Use the mouse to position the images a small distance apart. By crossing your eyes bring the numbers and images together.
Concentrate on bringing the numbers into focus at which point the images should make a plus sign.
Once in focus, slowly move the mouse and keep the numbers together and in focus.
The farther apart you spread the images and retain focus the more your eyes cross as if you were looking at a very close object.
You should notice an illusion that the center image is between you and the screen.
Distance vision procedure:
Click on the screen to activate image movement. The number indicates how far apart the images are.
Use the mouse to position the images a small distance apart. Relax your eyes and imagine gazing past the images into the distance..
You should see multiple images appearing. Try to bring the numbers together in the center and into focus.
Once in focus, slowly move the mouse and keep the numbers together and in focus.
The farther apart you spread the images and retain focus the more your eyes focus into the distance.
You should notice an illusion that the center image is in the distance.
